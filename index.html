    Delicacies

    Bingka

    "Bingka sa Mabinay" is enjoyed as a popular snack or dessert among locals and visitors alike, offering a taste of the region's culinary heritage. Travelers exploring Manjuyod may have the opportunity to sample this delicious treat at local bakeries, markets, or restaurants, providing a flavorful glimpse into the rich food culture of the area.

    Ilog River

    The Ilog River in Mabinay, Negros Oriental, is known for its natural beauty and is a part of the town’s small tourist industry. Activities such as kayaking and organized trips to many caves in the region are popular among visitors1. The river and its tributaries offer a scenic experience for those looking to appreciate nature’s charm.

    Inasal na Manok

    Chicken inasal is a popular Filipino dish known for its savory and slightly sweet marinade, often made with a mixture of vinegar, calamansi juice, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, and annatto oil, which gives the chicken its signature orange hue. The chicken is typically grilled over charcoal, imparting a smoky flavor.
